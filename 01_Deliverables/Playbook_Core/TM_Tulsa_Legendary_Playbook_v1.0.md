# TM_Tulsa_Legendary_Playbook_v1.0

## Purpose

The Tulsa Club Legendary Playbook defines the operational doctrine for building and sustaining a high-quality Toastmasters club.

It serves as a centralized reference for:

- club officers
- members
- future leadership teams

The playbook ensures continuity of excellence across leadership transitions.

---

# Vision

The Tulsa Club strives to be a **legendary Toastmasters club** known for:

- exceptional meetings
- strong member growth
- welcoming guest experiences
- leadership development
- consistent organizational systems

---

# Core Objectives

The playbook supports four primary objectives.

1. Consistent meeting excellence  
2. Member communication growth  
3. Leadership development  
4. Sustainable club growth

---

# Operating Philosophy

The club is built around several guiding principles:

- preparation improves meeting quality
- feedback drives improvement
- hospitality supports growth
- leadership must be developed
- systems protect the club

---

# Key Systems

The Legendary Playbook organizes the club around several core systems.

### Meeting System
Defines how meetings are structured and executed.

### Leadership System
Defines officer responsibilities and leadership development.

### Growth System
Defines guest acquisition, conversion, and retention.

### Culture System
Defines the values and behaviors that sustain the club environment.

---

# Intended Use

The playbook should be used by:

- club officers during planning
- members preparing for meeting roles
- new leaders learning club operations
- future officer teams maintaining continuity

---

# Living Document

The Legendary Playbook is intended to evolve as the club grows and improves.

Updates should reflect:

- lessons learned
- improved systems
- new initiatives
- leadership insights

Maintaining and improving the playbook is part of stewarding the club’s legacy.
