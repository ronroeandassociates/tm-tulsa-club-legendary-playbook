# Template_Officer_Transition_Checklist_v1.0

## Purpose

This checklist helps ensure smooth leadership transitions between outgoing and incoming officers.

---

# Transition Meeting

Schedule a transition meeting between outgoing and incoming officer.

Review responsibilities of the role.

Discuss current club priorities.

---

# Documentation Transfer

Provide access to:

Club email accounts

Club website

Member management systems

Meeting agendas

Operational documents

---

# Key Contacts

Share important contacts:

District leadership

Club officers

Venue contacts

Club mentors or coaches

---

# Recurring Responsibilities

Explain recurring tasks such as:

Meeting preparation

Agenda management

Member communication

Officer meetings

District reporting

---

# Current Projects

Review any active initiatives such as:

membership drives

open houses

special events

club improvements

---

# Lessons Learned

Outgoing officer should share:

what worked well

common challenges

recommendations for improvement

---

# Confirmation

Incoming officer confirms access to:

systems

documents

communication channels

---

# Completion

Transition complete when incoming officer is prepared to operate independently.
