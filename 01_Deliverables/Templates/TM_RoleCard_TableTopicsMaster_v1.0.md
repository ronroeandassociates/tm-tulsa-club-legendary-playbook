# Table Topics Master Role Card

## Mission
Lead the impromptu speaking session.

## Preparation
Prepare 5–8 short prompts.

Good prompts are:
• Clear
• Engaging
• Open-ended

## During Session

1. Explain Table Topics
2. Call participants
3. Deliver prompt
4. Invite speaker to respond

## Time
Typically 1–2 minutes.

## Key Principle
Encourage participation and confidence.
