# Timer Role Card

## Mission
Ensure speeches stay within required time limits.

## Timing Signals

Green — minimum time reached  
Yellow — approaching maximum  
Red — maximum time reached

## During Each Speech
1. Start timing when speaker begins.
2. Display timing signals.
3. Record final time.

## Report
At the end of the meeting report all times.

Example:
"Speaker John Smith — 6 minutes 12 seconds."

## Key Principle
Accuracy and visibility.
