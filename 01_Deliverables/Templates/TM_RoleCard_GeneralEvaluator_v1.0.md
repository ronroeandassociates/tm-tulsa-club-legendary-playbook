# General Evaluator Role Card

## Mission
Evaluate the entire meeting.

## Before Meeting
Meet with evaluation team.

Confirm:
• Timer
• Ah-Counter
• Grammarian
• Speech Evaluators

## During Meeting
Observe:
• Meeting flow
• Timing
• Role performance

## Evaluation Report
Provide summary of:

• Strengths
• Opportunities for improvement

## Key Principle
Constructive and balanced feedback.
