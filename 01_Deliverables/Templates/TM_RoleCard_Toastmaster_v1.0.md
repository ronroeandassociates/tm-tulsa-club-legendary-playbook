# Toastmaster Role Card

## Mission
Guide the meeting smoothly and introduce speakers clearly.

## Before the Meeting
• Review agenda  
• Confirm speaker names and pronunciation  
• Collect speech titles and introductions  
• Understand meeting theme

## During the Meeting

### Opening
Briefly introduce the meeting theme.

### Prepared Speeches
For each speaker:

1. Read speech introduction
2. Invite speaker to stage
3. Thank speaker afterward

### Transitions
Keep comments short and maintain flow.

### Hand Off
Transition to the next meeting section.

## Key Principle
Facilitate the meeting — do not dominate it.
