# Ah-Counter Role Card

## Mission
Help members become aware of filler words.

Track:
• Ah
• Um
• So
• Like
• Repeated phrases
• Verbal pauses

## During Meeting
Record fillers for each speaker.

## Report
Provide a brief summary.

Example:
"John used 3 ums and 2 filler phrases."

## Key Principle
Encourage awareness, not criticism.
