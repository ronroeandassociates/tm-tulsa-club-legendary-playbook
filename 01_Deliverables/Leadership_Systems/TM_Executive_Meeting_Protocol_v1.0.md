# TM_Executive_Meeting_Protocol_v1.0

## Purpose

This protocol defines the **standard operating rhythm** for executive committee meetings of Tulsa Club Toastmasters. The goal is to ensure meetings remain focused, efficient, and decision-oriented.

Executive meetings should prioritize **clarity, accountability, and forward progress** rather than long status discussions.

Target meeting length: **30–40 minutes**.

---

## Executive Meeting Flow

### 1. Control Board Review (2 minutes)

The President begins the meeting by reviewing the **Executive Control Board**.

Key indicators reviewed:

* Active membership
* Guest pipeline
* Speaker pipeline
* Financial status
* Distinguished Club progress

Purpose: establish **situational awareness** for the leadership team.

---

### 2. Officer Dashboard Updates (7–10 minutes)

Each officer provides a **concise 60‑second update** using their dashboard.

| Officer             | Focus                                  |
| ------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| VP Education        | Speaker pipeline and meeting readiness |
| VP Membership       | Guest pipeline and conversions         |
| VP Public Relations | Marketing visibility                   |
| Treasurer           | Financial health                       |
| Secretary           | Records and compliance                 |
| Sergeant‑at‑Arms    | Meeting logistics                      |

Reports should emphasize **exceptions, risks, or notable progress** rather than reading the dashboard.

---

### 3. Strategic Business Items (15–20 minutes)

The executive team focuses on **decisions and initiatives**.

Examples:

* membership growth campaigns
* open house planning
* speech contest preparation
* marketing initiatives
* member experience improvements

Each discussion should end with:

* a decision
* an owner
* a target completion date

---

### 4. Action Summary (3 minutes)

The Secretary confirms all action items.

| Action | Owner | Due Date |
| ------ | ----- | -------- |
|        |       |          |

This step ensures **clear accountability**.

---

### 5. Close (1 minute)

The President confirms:

* next executive meeting date
* any urgent follow‑ups

The meeting is then adjourned.

---

## Meeting Discipline Rules

To maintain efficiency, the executive team follows these rules:

1. Dashboard updates are limited to **60 seconds per officer**.
2. Discussions focus on **solutions and decisions**, not extended analysis.
3. Only executive officers participate in decision discussions unless guests are invited.
4. Action items must have **clear ownership and deadlines**.

---

## Expected Outcomes

When this protocol is followed consistently, executive meetings will:

* remain under 40 minutes
* maintain leadership alignment
* drive measurable progress toward club goals

This protocol supports the broader **Leadership Operating System** defined in the Legendary Playbook.
