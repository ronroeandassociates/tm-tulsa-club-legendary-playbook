# TM_VPM_Dashboard_v1.0

## Purpose

The Vice President Membership (VPM) Dashboard monitors the **membership growth engine** of the club. It tracks guest flow, follow‑up activity, conversion into membership, and retention of existing members.

The VPM should be able to answer quickly:

> Are we bringing in guests, converting them to members, and retaining our current members?
