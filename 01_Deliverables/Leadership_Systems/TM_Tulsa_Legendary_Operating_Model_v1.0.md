# TM_Tulsa_Legendary_Operating_Model_v1.0

## Purpose

The Tulsa Legendary Operating Model describes how the Tulsa Club Toastmasters system functions as an integrated operating framework. It connects meetings, member development, leadership governance, and performance outcomes into one coherent structure.

This model helps officers understand **how daily club activity leads to long‑term club success**.

---

## Core Principle

The club operates as a reinforcing cycle:

1. Members participate in meetings.
2. Meetings create educational progress.
3. Educational progress builds engagement.
4. Engagement strengthens membership growth.
5. Leadership systems maintain consistency and quality.
6. Consistency produces Distinguished Club performance.

This cycle repeats continuously throughout the year.

---

## The Operating Cycle

```
Guests → Members → Meetings → Education Progress → Engagement → Leadership Systems → Distinguished Club Success
```

Each stage supports the next.

---

## System Layers

### 1. Member Experience Layer

The foundation of the club.

Components:

* welcoming environment
* engaging meetings
* opportunities to speak and lead
* constructive feedback

Key roles:

* Sergeant‑at‑Arms
* Toastmaster of the Day
* Evaluators

Goal: **create a positive and professional meeting environment**.

---

### 2. Education Pipeline

The educational system ensures members progress through the Toastmasters Pathways program.

Components:

* prepared speeches
* evaluations
* Pathways project completion
* leadership opportunities

Primary responsibility:

* Vice President Education

Goal: **consistent member growth and skill development**.

---

### 3. Membership Growth Engine

The membership system brings new people into the club and retains existing members.

Components:

* guest invitations
* guest follow‑ups
* open houses
* member referrals

Primary responsibility:

* Vice President Membership

Goal: **maintain a healthy and growing membership base**.

---

### 4. Visibility and Outreach

The club maintains an external presence in the community.

Components:

* social media
* website presence
* promotional campaigns
* event promotion

Primary responsibility:

* Vice President Public Relations

Goal: **attract prospective guests and strengthen community awareness**.

---

### 5. Leadership Governance

Leadership systems ensure the club operates smoothly and decisions are made effectively.

Components:

* Executive Control Board
* Officer Dashboards
* Executive Meeting Protocol
* Officer Reporting Standard

Primary responsibility:

* Club President

Goal: **maintain coordination, accountability, and operational clarity**.

---

### 6. Performance Tracking

The club measures success through the Distinguished Club Program.

Components:

* education achievements
* membership growth
* officer training
* administrative compliance

Tracking tool:

* Distinguished Club Command Panel

Goal: **achieve Select Distinguished, President’s Distinguished or Smedley Distinguished status**.

---

## The Legendary Club Outcome

When all layers operate effectively, the club produces:

* consistent high‑quality meetings
* strong member engagement
* steady membership growth
* leadership development
* Distinguished Club recognition

This is the operational definition of a **Legendary Toastmasters Club**.

---

## How Officers Use This Model

Officers should view this model as the **system architecture** of the club.

Questions to ask regularly:

* Are meetings engaging and well organized?
* Are members progressing through Pathways?
* Are guests visiting and converting to members?
* Are leadership systems functioning effectively?

When all areas are healthy, the club will naturally progress toward Distinguished status.
