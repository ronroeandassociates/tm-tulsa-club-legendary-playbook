# TM_VPPR_Dashboard_v1.0

## Purpose

The Vice President Public Relations (VPPR) Dashboard tracks the **visibility and marketing effectiveness** of the club. It helps ensure the club maintains a consistent public presence, promotes meetings and events, and attracts prospective guests.

The VPPR should be able to answer quickly:

> Are we visible in the community and consistently promoting the club to attract guests?
