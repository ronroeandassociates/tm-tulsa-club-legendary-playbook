# TM_President_Dashboard_v1.0

## Purpose

The President Dashboard provides the strategic leadership view of the club. It helps the President monitor club health, coordinate officer leadership, and ensure alignment with Distinguished Club goals.

This dashboard should allow the President to quickly answer:

> Is the club functioning well, and where should leadership attention be focused?

## President Responsibilities During Executive Meetings

1. Open the meeting by reviewing the Executive Control Board.
2. Confirm officer reports are concise and focused.
3. Guide discussion toward decisions and actions.
4. Ensure responsibilities and deadlines are assigned.
5. Close with a summary of next steps.

---

## Leadership Philosophy

The President’s role is not to run every aspect of the club but to coordinate leadership, maintain culture, and ensure progress.

Effective presidents focus on:

* empowering officers
* maintaining meeting quality
* supporting member growth
* guiding the club toward Distinguished status
