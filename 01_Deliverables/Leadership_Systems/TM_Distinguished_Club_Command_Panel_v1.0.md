# TM_Distinguished_Club_Command_Panel_v1.0

## Purpose

The Distinguished Club Command Panel tracks progress toward the annual **Distinguished Club Program (DCP)** goals. It connects day‑to‑day club activity with long‑term performance targets.

This page allows the executive team to quickly answer:

> Are we on track to achieve Distinguished, Select Distinguished, or President’s Distinguished status this year?

The panel should be reviewed during **monthly executive meetings** and updated as achievements occur.

---

## Distinguished Club Program Overview

The Distinguished Club Program measures club performance across three categories:

1. **Education Achievements**
2. **Membership Growth**
3. **Administrative & Training Goals**

Clubs earn recognition based on the number of goals completed.

| Recognition Level              | Goals Required |
| ------------------------------ | -------------- |
| Distinguished Club             | 5 goals        |
| Select Distinguished Club      | 7 goals        |
| President’s Distinguished Club | 9 goals        |

## Executive Team Usage

During executive meetings:

1. The President reviews the current goal count.
2. The VPE confirms education progress.
3. The VPM confirms membership progress.
4. The team identifies the **next achievable goals**.

The objective is to maintain steady progress throughout the year rather than rushing near the end of the program cycle.

---

## Strategic Use

The Distinguished Club Command Panel helps the leadership team:

* focus on the next achievable goal
* align officer activities
* maintain momentum during the year
* celebrate member achievements

This panel serves as the **performance scoreboard** for the club.
