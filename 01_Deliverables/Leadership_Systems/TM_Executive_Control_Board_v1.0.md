# TM_Executive_Control_Board_v1.0

## Purpose

The Executive Control Board is the primary leadership dashboard for Tulsa Club Toastmasters. It provides the President and executive team with a single-page operational view of club health across all officer functions.

The board is reviewed at the beginning of every executive committee meeting to establish situational awareness before decisions are made.

Guiding principle:

> Updates should take less than two minutes. Discussion should focus on actions and decisions.

## Executive Meeting Usage

### Step 1 — President Opens Meeting

The President briefly reviews the control board metrics.

Example items:

* Active members
* Guest visits
* Speaker pipeline
* Financial status

### Step 2 — Officer Clarifications

Officers provide short clarifications only when needed.

Examples:

* VPE confirms upcoming speaker slots
* Treasurer confirms dues status
* VPM confirms guest follow-ups

### Step 3 — Move to Decisions

Once the board is reviewed, the meeting moves directly to decisions such as:

* membership initiatives
* upcoming events
* operational improvements
