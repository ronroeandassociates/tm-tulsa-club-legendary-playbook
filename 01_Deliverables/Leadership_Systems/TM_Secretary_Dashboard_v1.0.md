# TM_Secretary_Dashboard_v1.0

## Purpose

The Secretary Dashboard tracks **club records, documentation, and administrative compliance**. It ensures meeting minutes are recorded, membership records are accurate, and important documents are archived for the club.

The Secretary should be able to answer quickly:

> Are our records current and is the club administratively up to date?

## Secretary Executive Report (Typical 60‑Second Update)

Example summary:

* Last meeting minutes completed and distributed
* Membership roster updated with two new members
