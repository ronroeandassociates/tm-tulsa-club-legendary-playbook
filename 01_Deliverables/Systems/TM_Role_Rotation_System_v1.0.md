# Purpose

The Role Rotation System tracks member participation across meeting roles to ensure:

- fair distribution of opportunities
- steady member development
- balanced speaking and evaluation cycles
- predictable agenda preparation

The system supports the Vice President Education when assigning meeting roles and generating agendas.

---

# Core Principles

The Role Rotation System follows four principles.

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
Balance | members rotate through different roles |
Development | new members gain speaking experience early |
Mentorship | experienced members evaluate and mentor |
Preparation | roles are assigned with sufficient notice |

Consistent rotation prevents overuse of the same members.

- General Evaluator

## Program Leadership

- Table Topics Master

## Support Roles

- Grammarian

These categories help maintain balanced development.

---

# Speaking Rotation

Members should have regular speaking opportunities.

Suggested target:

| Experience Level | Suggested Frequency |
|------------------|---------------------|
New members | every 4–6 weeks |
Active members | every 6–8 weeks |
Experienced members | every 8–12 weeks |

These ranges ensure members progress through the education program.

---

# Evaluation Rotation

Evaluations should occur frequently because they build analytical communication skills.

Suggested rotation:

| Experience Level | Suggested Frequency |
|------------------|---------------------|
New members | after first speech |
Active members | every 4–6 weeks |
Experienced members | every 3–4 weeks |

Evaluations help strengthen the entire club.

---

# Leadership Role Rotation

Leadership roles help members develop meeting facilitation skills.

Roles include:

- Toastmaster
- Table Topics Master
- General Evaluator

Suggested frequency:

Every member should perform at least one leadership role per quarter.

---

# Role Assignment Guidelines

When assigning roles, the VP Education should consider:

| Factor | Reason |
|-------|-------|
Role history | maintain rotation balance |
Experience level | assign appropriate roles |
Member goals | support Pathways progress |
Availability | ensure reliable participation |

The goal is development, not simply filling roles.

---

# AI Integration

The Role Rotation System supports AI-assisted agenda generation.

Inputs include:

- member role history
- upcoming speakers
- member availability
- experience levels

AI can use this information to generate balanced agendas.

Example prompt input:

Members list with role history  
Next meeting speakers  
Members unavailable

The AI produces a suggested agenda that respects role rotation.
