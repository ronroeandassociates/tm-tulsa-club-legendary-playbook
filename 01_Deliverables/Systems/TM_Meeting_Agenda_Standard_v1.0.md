# Purpose

The Meeting Agenda Standard defines the structure used for all regular Tulsa Club Toastmasters meetings.

A consistent agenda ensures:

- meetings start and end on time
- each role is clearly defined
- guests understand the program
- members receive consistent speaking opportunities

This system supports smooth meeting execution.

---

# Meeting Time Standard

| Item | Time |
|-----|------|
Doors Open | 6:15 PM |
Meeting Start | 6:30 PM |
Meeting End | 7:45 PM |
Networking | 7:45–8:00 PM |

Total program time: **75 minutes**

---

# Standard Meeting Structure

| Segment | Duration |
|--------|---------|
Opening | 5 min |
Table Topics | 10–15 min |
Prepared Speeches | 20–25 min |
Evaluation | 20 min |
Closing | 3–5 min |

# Agenda Preparation Timeline

| Timeline | Task |
|--------|------|
3 weeks before | schedule speakers |
1 week before | confirm meeting roles |
24 hours before | finalize speech details |

Speakers should submit the following information on the website:

- Path
- Level
- Project
- Speech Title
- Speaker Introduction

This information appears on the **agenda and meeting website**.

---

# Meeting Segment Overview

## Opening

Conducted by the Presiding Officer.

Activities include:

- call meeting to order
- welcome guests
- introduce Toastmaster

---

## Table Topics

Led by the Table Topics Master.

Purpose:

- practice impromptu speaking
- encourage member participation
- allow guests to experience Toastmasters

---

## Prepared Speeches

Members deliver prepared speeches aligned with Pathways projects.

Each speaker is introduced by the Toastmaster.

---

## Evaluation

Led by the General Evaluator.

Includes:

- speech evaluations
- Timer report
- Grammarian report
- Ah-Counter report
- overall meeting evaluation

---

## Closing

Conducted by the Presiding Officer.

Activities include:

- final announcements
- guest recognition
- meeting adjournment

---

# Agenda Quality Standards

A high-quality agenda ensures:

- speakers are scheduled early
- evaluators understand their roles
- speech titles are confirmed
- guests can follow the program

Consistency improves meeting professionalism.
