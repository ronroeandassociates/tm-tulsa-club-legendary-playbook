# Purpose

The Agenda Automation System assists the Vice President Education in scheduling meeting roles and preparing the agenda.

The goal is to:

- reduce manual scheduling work
- ensure balanced participation
- maintain consistent meeting quality
- provide preparation time for speakers and evaluators

AI tools may assist with generating role assignments and agenda drafts.

---

# Agenda Planning Timeline

The agenda is planned on a rolling basis.

| Timeline | Task |
|--------|------|
3 weeks before | assign speakers |
2 weeks before | assign evaluators |
1 week before | assign remaining roles |
24 hours before | finalize speech details |

This timeline ensures members have sufficient preparation time.

---

# Agenda Role Categories

Roles are divided into three categories.

## Program Leadership

- Toastmaster
- Table Topics Master
- General Evaluator

## Speaking Roles

- Prepared Speakers
- Speech Evaluators

## Support Roles

- Timer
- Grammarian
- Ah-Counter
- Sergeant at Arms

Balanced participation across these roles supports member development.

---

# Participation Balance

The system should ensure:

- members rotate through roles
- new members receive early speaking opportunities
- experienced members mentor through evaluation roles

Members should avoid repeating the same role frequently.

---

# Data Required for Automation

To assist with agenda generation, the following information should be tracked.

| Field | Purpose |
|-----|------|
Member name | role assignment |
Last speaking date | speaking rotation |
Last evaluation date | evaluation balance |
Experience level | appropriate role assignment |
Availability | meeting participation |

This information can be maintained in a simple spreadsheet.

---

# AI Agenda Generation

AI tools can assist the VP Education by generating draft agendas.

Inputs include:

- member list
- recent role history
- upcoming speakers
- availability

Example prompt structure:

Generate a Toastmasters meeting agenda using the following conditions:

Meeting date: [date]  
Speakers already scheduled: [names]  
Members available: [list]  

Ensure that:

- members are not assigned the same role repeatedly
- experienced members evaluate newer speakers
- all roles are filled

The output should include a full agenda.

# Website Agenda Entry

After the agenda is finalized, the VP Education enters roles into the club website.

Speakers should enter the following information on the website:

- Path
- Level
- Project
- Speech Title
- Speaker Introduction

This information appears on the meeting agenda.

---

# Agenda Quality Controls

Before publishing the agenda, verify:

- all roles assigned
- evaluators matched with speakers
- speakers confirmed
- speech titles submitted

A complete agenda prevents meeting delays.

---

# Benefits of Agenda Automation

Using structured scheduling provides several benefits:

- reduced administrative workload

- better meeting preparation
- increased member engagement

Automation allows the VP Education to focus more on member development.
