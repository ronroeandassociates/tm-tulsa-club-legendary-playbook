# Purpose

The Sergeant at Arms prepares the meeting environment and supports meeting logistics.

This role ensures the meeting space is organized and ready for members and guests.

---

# Core Responsibilities

The Sergeant at Arms is responsible for:

- preparing the meeting room
- setting up the Toastmasters banner
- arranging meeting materials
- welcoming members and guests
- assisting with room cleanup

The role supports a professional meeting environment.

---

# Preparation

Preparation should occur before members arrive.

Tasks include:

- reviewing the meeting agenda
- confirming room layout
- preparing meeting materials

This role ensures the meeting environment is ready.

---

# Arrival

The Sergeant at Arms should arrive **20–30 minutes before the meeting**.

Arrival tasks include:

- preparing the speaking area
- placing the Toastmasters banner
- setting up guest sign-in
- preparing timing equipment
- placing agendas if printed

Early preparation creates a welcoming environment.

---

# Room Setup

The Sergeant at Arms should follow the **Room Layout Standard**.

Key setup areas:

- speaking area
- audience tables
- guest sign-in
- hospitality area

Tables typically remain in their standard configuration.

---

# Guest Welcome

The Sergeant at Arms may assist with welcoming guests.

Responsibilities include:

- greeting guests at the entrance
- directing them to sign-in
- helping them find seating

Guest comfort is important to the club experience.

---

# Meeting Support

During the meeting the Sergeant at Arms may:

- assist with late arrivals
- distribute materials
- help maintain order

The role supports meeting flow without interrupting speakers.

---

# Post-Meeting Cleanup

After the meeting the Sergeant at Arms coordinates cleanup.

Tasks include:

- removing materials
- clearing hospitality tables
- restoring room layout
- ensuring the venue is left in good condition

Cleanup usually takes **10–15 minutes**.

---

# Integration with Systems

This role supports several club systems:

- TM_Room_Layout_Standard_v1.0.md
- TM_Hospitality_System_v1.0.md
- TM_Guest_Experience_System_v1.0.md

These systems ensure a consistent meeting environment.

---

# Common Failure Points

| Issue | Prevention |
|------|-------------|
Room not ready | arrive early |
Banner missing | include setup checklist |
Guest confusion | provide clear sign-in area |
Cleanup incomplete | assign volunteers |

---

# Success Indicators

The Sergeant at Arms is successful when:

- the room is prepared before members arrive
- guests are welcomed comfortably
- meeting logistics run smoothly
- the venue is restored after the meeting
