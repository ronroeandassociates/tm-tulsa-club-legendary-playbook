Meeting Role Script

Grammarian

The Grammarian supports clear, effective language throughout the meeting and introduces the "Word of the Day" to encourage vocabulary growth.

Role Purpose

The Grammarian helps members improve language usage, clarity, and vocabulary. This role encourages stronger communication while reinforcing good speaking habits.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Introduce the Word of the Day
  • Listen for effective language usage
  • Note grammar improvements when appropriate
  • Encourage members to use the Word of the Day

What Good Looks Like

  • The word of the day is clear and easy to use
  • Your listening is active and attentive
  • Your feedback is constructive
  • Your report is brief and helpful

Word of the Day

Choose a word that is simple enough to use naturally but uncommon enough to encourage growth.

  • Select a word with clear meaning
  • Provide pronunciation and definition
  • Use the word in a sentence
  • Encourage members to incorporate it during the meeting

Sample Script

Introduction:

"As Grammarian, I will listen for effective language, clear phrasing, and strong word usage throughout the meeting. I will also introduce today's Word of the Day."

Word of the Day example:

"Today's Word of the Day is 'articulate.' Articulate means expressing ideas clearly and effectively. For example: 'She delivered an articulate presentation that captured everyone's attention.' I encourage everyone to use the word articulate during today's meeting."

Report example:

"Several speakers used today's Word of the Day effectively. Andrew used it during Table Topics, and Wanda incorporated it in her evaluation. Overall, language usage during the meeting was strong and engaging."

Quick Tips

Choose a Simple Word

Words that are easy to incorporate encourage participation.

Listen for Strengths

Highlight strong language choices, not just mistakes.

Keep the Report Short

Your summary should be concise and encouraging.
