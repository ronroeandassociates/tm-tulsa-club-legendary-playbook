Meeting Role Script

General Evaluator

The General Evaluator leads the evaluation portion of the meeting and helps ensure that feedback is meaningful, organized, and helpful for all participants.

Role Purpose

The General Evaluator oversees the entire evaluation team and provides an overall assessment of the meeting's effectiveness.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Introduce the evaluation team
  • Ensure evaluators understand their responsibilities
  • Evaluate the meeting as a whole
  • Call for reports from Timer, Ah‑Counter, and Grammarian

What Good Looks Like

  • The evaluation portion runs smoothly
  • Each evaluator provides useful feedback
  • Your meeting evaluation highlights both strengths and improvements
  • The tone remains positive and supportive

Preparation Before the Meeting

  1. Review the meeting agenda.
  2. Confirm the evaluation team members.
  3. Ensure each evaluator knows the speaker they are assigned.
  4. Prepare notes on what to observe during the meeting.

What to Observe

Meeting Flow

  • Were transitions smooth?
  • Did the meeting stay on schedule?

Meeting Roles

  • Were roles clearly executed?
  • Did each participant understand their role?

Member Engagement

  • Were members attentive?
  • Did guests feel welcomed?

Sample Script

Introduction:

"As General Evaluator, I will oversee the evaluation portion of today's meeting and ensure that each speaker receives helpful feedback."

"I would like to introduce today's evaluation team: our Timer, Ah‑Counter, and Grammarian."

Meeting Evaluation Example:

"Overall, the meeting ran smoothly and stayed close to schedule. Transitions between segments were clear, and speakers maintained strong engagement with the audience. One area to improve would be allowing slightly more pause time between speakers so the audience can fully absorb the message."

Quick Tips

Lead the Evaluation Team

Your role is to coordinate the feedback portion of the meeting.

Observe the Whole Meeting

Think beyond individual speeches and consider the meeting experience overall.

Stay Positive

Constructive feedback encourages growth and confidence.
