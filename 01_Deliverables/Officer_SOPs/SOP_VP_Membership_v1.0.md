# Purpose

The Vice President Membership (VPM) leads the club’s membership growth and guest engagement efforts.

The VPM ensures that guests are welcomed, supported, and encouraged to become members.

This role focuses on:

- guest experience
- membership recruitment
- membership retention

A healthy membership pipeline supports the long-term success of the club.

---

# Core Responsibilities

The Vice President Membership is responsible for:

- welcoming and tracking guests
- following up with visitors
- promoting membership opportunities
- assisting guests with joining the club
- supporting new member integration

The VPM ensures that every guest receives a positive experience.

---

# Guest Experience Leadership

The VPM oversees the **Guest Experience System**.

Key responsibilities include:

- ensuring guests are greeted upon arrival
- coordinating guest ambassadors
- distributing guests across tables
- ensuring guests feel included during meetings

The first visit experience strongly influences whether guests return.

---

# Guest Sign-In Management

Guests should sign in when they arrive.

Information typically collected includes:

| Field | Purpose |
|------|--------|
Name | guest identification |
Email | follow-up communication |
Phone | optional contact |
How they heard about the club | recruitment insight |

The VPM maintains guest records for follow-up.

---

# Guest Follow-Up

Follow-up should occur **within 24 hours of the meeting**.

Typical follow-up communication includes:

- thanking the guest for attending
- inviting them to return
- providing information about membership

Prompt follow-up significantly increases conversion rates.

---

# Membership Invitations

The VPM should invite guests to join when appropriate.

This may occur:

- during Open House events
- after guest participation
- during follow-up communication

The invitation should explain:

- membership benefits
- meeting schedule
- dues structure
- how to join

Membership forms should be readily available.

---

# Open House Coordination

The VPM typically leads **Open House events**.

Responsibilities include:

- coordinating event promotion
- welcoming guests
- explaining membership opportunities
- managing follow-up communication

Open House meetings are key recruitment events.

---

# New Member Integration

Once a guest becomes a member, the VPM helps integrate them into the club.

Integration activities include:

- welcoming the new member publicly
- introducing them to other members
- encouraging meeting role participation
- coordinating mentor assignments when applicable

Strong integration improves member retention.

---

# Membership Tracking

The VPM monitors membership levels including:

- total membership count
- guest attendance
- membership conversions
- member retention

Tracking helps the club maintain healthy membership.

---

# Membership Goals

Typical membership goals may include:

| Goal | Target |
|------|-------|
Guests per meeting | 1–3 |
Open House guests | 10+ |
Membership conversion | 20–40% |

These metrics help measure recruitment success.

---

- TM_Guest_Experience_System_v1.0.md
- SOP_Open_House_Event_v1.0.md
- TM_Table_Participation_System_v1.0.md

---

# Collaboration with Other Officers

The VPM works closely with:

| Officer | Collaboration |
|--------|---------------|
VP Education | helping new members obtain speaking roles |
VP Public Relations | promoting guest events |
President | monitoring membership health |

Collaboration strengthens recruitment efforts.

---

# Common Failure Points

| Issue | Prevention |
|------|-------------|
Guests not greeted | assign guest ambassadors |
No follow-up | send follow-up within 24 hours |
Guests feel excluded | distribute guests across tables |
New members disengage | integrate quickly into meeting roles |

---

# Success Indicators

The VPM is successful when:

- guests feel welcomed
- guests return for additional meetings
- membership steadily grows
- new members remain engaged

---

# Officer Transition

At the end of the term the VPM should:

- transfer guest contact records
- review membership trends
- share recruitment strategies

Strong transitions maintain membership momentum.
