# Purpose

The Vice President Public Relations (VPPR) manages the club’s communication with the public.

The VPPR promotes the club, maintains the website, and communicates club activities to members and the broader community.

The goal of this role is to ensure the club remains visible, welcoming, and easy for guests to discover.

---

# Core Responsibilities

The Vice President Public Relations is responsible for:

- maintaining the club website
- promoting club meetings and events
- supporting membership recruitment efforts
- communicating club achievements
- managing social media and digital communications

This role helps attract guests and build the club’s reputation.

---

# Website Management

The VPPR maintains the club website.

Platform:

FreeToastHost

Domains typically include:

- tulsatoastmasters.org
- tulsatoastmasters.club

The VPPR ensures the website contains accurate and up-to-date information.

Key information includes:

| Content | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
Meeting time | current |
Meeting location | accurate |
Guest invitation | visible |
Event announcements | updated |
Officer list | current |

Website accuracy is essential for guest discovery.

---

# Monthly Website Review

The VPPR should review the website monthly.

Checklist:

- verify meeting time and location
- confirm officer information
- update event announcements
- verify contact links

Regular reviews prevent outdated information.

---

# Event Promotion

The VPPR promotes club events including:

- Open House meetings
- speech contests
- anniversary events
- leadership showcase meetings

Promotion may include:

- website updates
- social media posts
- email announcements

Effective promotion increases attendance.

---

# Social Media

The VPPR may manage social media channels.

Typical uses include:

- promoting upcoming meetings
- sharing member achievements
- announcing events
- encouraging guest attendance

Social media should remain positive and professional.

---

# Collaboration with VP Membership

The VPPR works closely with the Vice President Membership to support guest acquisition.

Collaboration includes:

- promoting Open House events
- encouraging guest attendance
- sharing membership opportunities

Together these roles support club growth.

---

# Club Announcements

The VPPR may assist with communication such as:

- event reminders
- club milestone announcements
- district event promotion

Communication should remain clear and concise.

---

# Communication Standards

All public communication should:

- represent the club professionally
- maintain positive messaging
- accurately reflect meeting information

Professional communication supports club credibility.

---

# Integration with Club Systems

The VPPR works closely with several club systems:

- TM_Website_Operations_System_v1.0.md
- TM_Guest_Experience_System_v1.0.md
- TM_Annual_Operating_Calendar_v1.0.md

These systems help ensure consistent public communication.

---

# Common Failure Points

| Issue | Prevention |
|------|-------------|
Outdated website | perform monthly review |
Events not promoted | coordinate with officers |
Inconsistent messaging | maintain clear communication |
Website neglected after officer transition | transfer access during transition |

---

# Success Indicators

The VPPR is successful when:

- guests easily find meeting information
- events receive strong attendance
- the club maintains positive public visibility

---

# Officer Transition

At the end of the term the VPPR should:

- transfer website access credentials
- review website update procedures
- identify any pages needing updates

Smooth transitions ensure continuity in club communications.
