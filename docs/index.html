How to Use This Page

Keep the workflow simple. The page should help a member find what they need in seconds, not force them to browse the repository structure.

1

Need a meeting role?

Go to Meeting Role Scripts and open the role you were assigned. Review it before the meeting or keep it open on your phone during the meeting.

2

Need officer guidance?

Open Officer Resources to find role-based procedures and continuity documentation for leadership operations.

3

Need event or template material?

Use Event Systems and Templates for open houses, club events, speech support, and reusable documents.
